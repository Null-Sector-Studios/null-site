Threshold

Threshold is a horror game created by Null Sector Studios. Inspired by Five Nights at Freddy’s, Indigo Park, DOORS, Poppy Playtime and SCP, you play a researcher for Kovenox Industries —a company that mastered portal travel using large stone gates. After a power surge rips open a portal to a mysterious dimension, entities pour out. In this chapter-based game you’ll uncover the secrets of the Threshold, one eerie level at a time.

Chapter Breakdown

  • Chapter 0: “Ground Zerø” – Demo on Steam & GameJolt.
  • Chapter 1: Echoes of Alpha 5 – Full game (free).
  • Chapters 2+ (TBA) – Paid expansions (pricing varies).

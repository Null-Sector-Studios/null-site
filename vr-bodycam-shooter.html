VR Bodycam Shooter (Temporary Name)

This is an early-concept VR first-person shooter viewed entirely through a body-worn camera. We’re exploring tactical combat scenarios, immersive environments, and real-time physics—all in VR. Details are coming soon as we prototype core mechanics.

Concept Features

  • Tactical VR combat from a first-person bodycam angle
  • Dynamic physics interactions and destructible environments
  • Multiplayer training sims and scenario planning
  • Immersive UI elements projected in-world
